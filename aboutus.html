About Us

FlexiTrade - Your Role Your Choice.


At FlexiTrade, we believe in the power of connection and the limitless possibilities it holds. Our platform is designed to bring together sellers, buyers, and delivery drivers, creating a seamless ecosystem where everyone can thrive.

Our Story


It all started with a vision to revolutionize the way people buy and sell goods. We recognized the need for a platform that not only connects buyers and sellers but also ensures efficient and reliable delivery.

Our Mission


Our mission is simple yet ambitious: to empower individuals by providing them with the tools and opportunities to become sellers, buyers, and delivery drivers, all within a single platform. We believe that everyone should have the chance to explore their entrepreneurial spirit, find great deals, and contribute to the seamless movement of goods.

How It Works


Our platform is designed to be user-friendly, ensuring that anyone can navigate and utilize its features with ease. Whether you're a seller looking to showcase your products, a buyer in search of unique finds, or a delivery driver seeking flexible earning opportunities, we've got you covered.

Seller


As a seller, you need to first get yourself registered as a seller after which our team will get in touch with you and verify you as a seller for you to begin selling products.

Buyer


For buyers, our platform offers a diverse marketplace filled with a wide range of products from various sellers. Discover new items, explore different categories, and make purchases with just a few clicks. You can track your order real time as it is being delivered to you by the person who took up the role of delivery driver.

Delivery Drivers


As a delivery driver we offer a flexible and rewarding way to earn income. For delivering an order, initially create a delivery driver profile and request for the deliveries that are available to deliver based on your comfort, once you are assigned a delivery just pickup the order from our warehouse and deliver it to the customer location. YES its that simple.
